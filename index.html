🌊 Urges are waves

They build, peak, and pass — usually within 10–20 minutes. Don't fight the wave; just don't act while it passes. Every wave you surf weakens the next one.

🚪 Change the scene

Bed + phone + alone + late is the classic trap. Stand up, leave the room, phone stays behind.

⚠️ Edging is the trap within the trap

Hours of elevated dopamine make it far more reinforcing than a quick slip — and it's the pattern most linked to PIED. If a slip starts, 5 minutes is far less damage than 5 hours.

↺ A slip is data, not doom

The streak resets; the progress doesn't. Log the trigger, learn one thing, skip the shame spiral — the spiral is the most dangerous part.

💞 Intimacy is the cure, not the test

Rough nights with PIED during the reboot are a symptom on its way out, not proof of failure. Watch the trend line, not any single night.

🧠 Rewiring is slow but real

The hardest stretch is the first 2–4 weeks — day 14 often feels flat and heavy on purpose. Real change comes over 60–90 days. That's why the constellation completes at day 90.
